## Understanding Sports Betting Types DataWise Covers

  

Before diving deeper into DataWise's features, it's important to understand the types of bets they help members with. DataWise covers virtually every major betting category:

  

### Primary Betting Markets

-   **Moneyline bets**: Straight up winner picks with no point spread involved
-   **Point spread**: Betting against the spread where favorites give points and underdogs receive points
-   **Over/under**: Total points/goals betting where you predict if the combined score goes over or under a set number

  

### Advanced Betting Strategies

-   **Parlay bets**: Multiple bets combined for higher payouts (but all must win)
-   **Teaser bets**: Modified parlays where you get better odds in exchange for lower payouts
-   **Proposition bets**: Specific outcome bets like "will Player X score over 20 points?"

  

### Professional-Level Markets

-   **Futures bets**: Long-term wagers like championship winners
-   **Live betting**: In-game wagering as events unfold
-   **Round robin bets**: Multiple parlay combinations from a set of picks

  

DataWise excels particularly at identifying +EV (positive expected value) opportunities across these markets, which is where serious bettors make their money.
